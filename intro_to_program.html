What is Health Coaching? 

Health coaching is the use of evidence-based based skillful conversation, clinical strategies, and interventions to actively and safely engage patients/families/clients in health behavior change to better self-manage their health, health risk(s), and acute or chronic health conditions resulting in optimal wellness and recovery, improved health outcomes, lowered health risk and decreased health care costs. 

Health coaches are credentialed medical professionals using the client’s condition and medical history to safely guide goal setting, and provide medical information as the need arises within the professional’s scope of practice. This coaching style is highly personalized and promotes insight-driven behavior change to help patients better focus on their health and well-being. Furthermore, such a valuable resource could result in patients having increased satisfaction with their doctors/care team, medication, and overall care. 

Digital Health Coaching is health coaching conveniently offered through an app. Digital Health Coaching can help easily and conveniently motivate patients and encourage them to become more engaged with their health and their life. With periodic check-ins, a digital health coaching program is designed to encourage long-term engagement and participation. Digital health coaching can be a great addition to existing disease management programs.

Reference:Huffman, M. Advancing the practice of health coaching: Differentiation from wellness coaching. Journal of Workplace Health & Safety, 64(9), 400-403.Huffman, M. (September 2016).
