Q: What is the relationship between my Hemophilia Health Coach and my Doctor/Care Team?

A: The connection with your Coach can optimize the interactions and care that you receive from your doctor/care team.

  • Your Coach will remind you to share information (such as every bleed, upcoming procedures, new medications, etc.) with your doctor. 
  • Your Coach can help you prepare for your doctor visits by: 
    • Helping verify the accuracy of your treatment logs, then assisting you with emailing or printing the logs for your doctor. 
    • Reminding you of questions, concerns or important topics to discuss at your doctorâ€™s visit. 
    • Helping you prepare a list of current medications for your doctor.  
  • Your Coach can not provide medical care, but can share information with you on relevant topics regarding your condition or concern. You can then use the information for more meaningful discussions with your doctor/care team. 

What your Coach can do for you:

  • Your Coach can help you prepare for doctor visits. 
    • Your coach cannot directly speak to your doctor or nurse on your behalf. 
  • Your Coach can help verify accuracy and completeness of your logging. 
    • Your coach cannot log your infusions or bleeds for you.  
  • Your Coach can help you prepare infusion/bleed logs to print or email to others. 
    • Your coach cannot send logs directly to your doctor, pharmacy, or insurance company.  
  • Your Coach can help you enter your personal information and treatment plan into the MicroHealth app.  
    • Your coach cannot access your account directly and enter this information for you.  
  • Your Coach may ask if you have enough medication supply on hand and ask the expiration date of your medication. Your Coach may want to view the prescription label and boxes to see your dose.  
    • Your coach cannot order medication refills for you or directly contact your pharmacy.  
  • Your Coach can help ensure you are prepared for emergencies. 
    • Your coach cannot be your resource during an emergency situation. 
  • Your Coach can give you information about: national and local foundations, advocacy groups, and your current pharmaceutical manufacturer. 
    • Your coach cannot share your personal or contact information with an organization or another person.
